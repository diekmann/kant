== Immanuel Kant

  From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


          Immanuel Kant (22 April 1724 – 12 February 1804)
            Born in Königsberg.
            German philosopher.
            Lived in the Age of Enlightenment.
            Famous for the Categorical Imperative.





























== Categorical Imperative


                 Act only according to that maxim whereby you can,
                 at the same time,
                 will that it should become a universal law.

































==  Maxim (philosophy)

  From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia


        A maxim is a concise expression of a fundamental moral rule or principle,
